Content Removal & Appeals

Effective Date: July 21, 2026

Our goal is not to remove content—it is to protect a positive community for runners. When moderation is necessary, we strive to be fair, consistent, and transparent.

Why Content May Be Removed

Content may be removed if it violates our Terms of Service, Community Guidelines, or applicable laws.

Examples include:

  • Harassment or bullying
  • Hate speech
  • Spam or scams
  • Sexually explicit content
  • Violence or threats
  • Illegal activity
  • Copyright infringement
  • Impersonation

Account Actions

Depending on the severity or frequency of violations, MileStory may:

  • Remove individual posts or comments.
  • Restrict account features.
  • Suspend an account temporarily.
  • Permanently terminate an account.

Appealing a Decision

If you believe your content or account was moderated in error, you may request a review by emailing:

milestoryapp@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your MileStory username
  • A description of the moderation action
  • Any additional information that may help us review your request

How Appeals Are Reviewed

Appeals are reviewed individually. We may uphold, modify, or reverse the original moderation decision after reviewing the available information.

Submitting an appeal does not guarantee that the original decision will change.

Repeat Violations

Repeated or serious violations may result in permanent account removal without additional warnings.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect improvements to our moderation processes or changes in applicable law.
