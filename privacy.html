Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2026

At MileStory, privacy isn't an afterthought—it's part of the product. We collect only the information needed to operate the app and help you share your running journey. We do not sell your personal information or use it for advertising.

MileStory ("MileStory," "we," "our," or "us") is a social platform that allows runners to document and share their training journey toward races and personal milestones. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Username
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Biography (optional)

Your password is securely handled by our authentication provider. MileStory never stores or has access to your password.

Content You Create

We store the content you choose to create, including:

  • Posts
  • Photos
  • Race journeys
  • Comments
  • Supports (likes)
  • Followers and following relationships

Content you share may be visible to other MileStory users depending on your privacy settings. Journeys may be public or private.

Connected Fitness Services

If you connect a supported fitness service such as Strava, MileStory imports only the information you authorize.

This may include:

  • Distance
  • Pace
  • Duration
  • Date
  • Route
  • Heart rate

MileStory never posts to your Strava account. You may disconnect your Strava account at any time, preventing future imports.

Information We Do Not Collect

MileStory does not:

  • Track your location in the background.
  • Access your contacts.
  • Collect advertising identifiers.
  • Sell your personal information.
  • Share your data for advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to operate and improve MileStory, including:

  • Displaying your profile and posts.
  • Showing content from people you follow.
  • Powering journeys and activity feeds.
  • Syncing authorized fitness activities.
  • Protecting your account.
  • Detecting abuse, spam, or fraudulent activity.
  • Responding to support requests.

Service Providers

MileStory uses trusted service providers to operate the app.

  • Supabase provides authentication, database hosting, and secure file storage.
  • Strava provides optional activity synchronization when you choose to connect your account.

These providers process data only as necessary to provide their services.

Data Retention

Your information is retained while your account remains active.

You may delete individual posts whenever you choose.

You may permanently delete your account from Settings → Delete Account.

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, posts, comments, journeys, follows, imported activities, and connected fitness-service credentials, except where retention is required by law or necessary for fraud prevention, security, or backup recovery.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Edit your profile.
  • Delete your posts.
  • Disconnect Strava.
  • Delete your account.
  • Contact us with privacy questions.

Children's Privacy

MileStory is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by local law).

If we learn that a child has created an account in violation of this policy, we will delete the account promptly.

Security

MileStory uses industry-standard safeguards, including secure authentication, encrypted connections, and database row-level security to help protect your information.

However, no online service can guarantee absolute security, and you use the Service at your own risk.

International Users

If you access MileStory outside the United States, you understand that your information may be processed and stored in countries where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When significant changes are made, we will update the Effective Date above and, where appropriate, notify users within the app.
