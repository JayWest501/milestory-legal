# MileStory Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 21 2026**

MileStory ("the app," "we," "us") is a social platform for runners to document and share their journey toward a race. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We built MileStory to be straightforward, and we've tried to keep this policy the same way.

## Information we collect

**Account information.** When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and username, along with a password handled by our authentication provider (we never see or store your password itself). You may optionally add a profile photo and bio.

**Content you create.** Posts, photos, race journeys, comments, supports (likes), and follows are stored so the app can display them to you and other users. Content you post is visible to other MileStory users; journeys can be set to public or private visibility.

**Run data you choose to import.** If you connect a fitness service such as Strava, we import activity data you authorize — such as distance, pace, duration, date, route, and heart rate — so you can attach it to your posts. We only access data you explicitly authorize through that service's permission screen, and we never post to your connected services. You can disconnect a service at any time in the app, which stops all future imports.

**Information we do not collect.** MileStory does not track your location in the background, does not access your contacts, does not collect advertising identifiers, and does not sell your data to anyone.

## How we use your information

We use your information solely to operate MileStory: displaying your profile and posts to other users, showing you content from runners and journeys you follow, powering features like the featured journey and activity notifications, and maintaining the security of your account. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not share it with third parties except the service providers described below.

## Service providers

MileStory runs on Supabase, which hosts our database, authentication, and file storage. If you connect Strava, data flows between Strava and MileStory according to the authorization you grant, under Strava's own terms and privacy policy. These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You can delete individual posts at any time. You can permanently delete your entire account in the app under Settings → Delete Account, which removes your profile, posts, journeys, comments, follows, imported activities, and connected-service credentials. Account deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

## Children

MileStory is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

## Security

Access to your data is protected by row-level security rules enforced at the database level, and credentials for connected fitness services are stored server-side and are never accessible from the app itself.

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and note the change in the app.
